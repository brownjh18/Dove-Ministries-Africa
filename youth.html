About Our Ministry

The Youth Ministry is dedicated to nurturing the next generation of Christian leaders. We create an environment where young adults can grow spiritually, develop their talents, and make a positive impact in their communities and the world.

Leadership Development

Training programs to develop leadership skills and prepare for future roles in ministry and society.

Community Service

Regular outreach programs and volunteer opportunities to serve the community.

Career Guidance

Mentorship and guidance for career development and life planning.

ABOUT US

The Dove Ministries Africa was founded in 2005 by Pastor Daniel and Erica Kaggwa, under the tutelage of Apostle Harry and Deborah Stackhouse. It is located in Nfuufu Zone, Zzana-Bunamwaya in Makindye-Ssabagabo municipality, about 500 metres from the Zzana Roundabout on Entebbe Road.
