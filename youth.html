Youth Ministry

Empowering young adults aged 18-35 to discover their purpose, build leadership skills, and serve their communities with passion and faith.

Learn More

Youth

Ecclesiastes 11:9

Rejoice, O young man, in your youth, and let your heart cheer you in the days of your youth. Walk in the ways of your heart and the sight of your eyes. But know that for all these things God will bring you into judgment.

The ministry offers a safe space to explore God's Word, discuss challenges, and grow in spiritual depth. Vibrant worship, engaging activities, and outreach initiatives foster a strong sense of belonging and service. The youth ministry empowers young hearts to stand firm in their beliefs, make a positive impact, and lead by example. Through friendship, laughter, and prayer, these young believers shine as a beacon of hope in their church and beyond..

Leadership Development

Training programs to develop leadership skills and prepare for future roles in ministry and society.

Community Service

Regular outreach programs and volunteer opportunities to serve the community.

Career Guidance

Mentorship and guidance for career development and life planning.

ABOUT US

The Dove Ministries Africa was founded in 2005 by Pastor Daniel and Erica Kaggwa, under the tutelage of Apostle Harry and Deborah Stackhouse. It is located in Nfuufu Zone, Zzana-Bunamwaya in Makindye-Ssabagabo municipality, about 500 metres from the Zzana Roundabout on Entebbe Road.
