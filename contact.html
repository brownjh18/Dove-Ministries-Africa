Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our ministry.

How can I get involved with your ministry?

You can get involved by volunteering, donating, participating in our events, or joining one of our local church communities. Contact us to learn about current opportunities.

Do you accept international volunteers?

Yes, we welcome international volunteers. We have structured programs for short-term and long-term volunteers. Please contact our volunteer coordinator for more information.

How are donations used?

Your donations support our various ministries including church planting, education programs, healthcare initiatives, and community development projects. We provide regular reports on how funds are used.

Can I visit your projects?

We welcome visitors to our projects and churches. Please contact us in advance to arrange a visit and learn about current opportunities to see our work firsthand.

ABOUT US

The Dove Ministries Africa was founded in 2005 by Pastor Daniel and Erica Kaggwa, under the tutelage of Apostle Harry and Deborah Stackhouse. It is located in Nfuufu Zone, Zzana-Bunamwaya in Makindye-Ssabagabo municipality, about 500 metres from the Zzana Roundabout on Entebbe Road.
