About Our Ministry

The Worship Ministry creates an atmosphere where God's presence can be experienced through music, dance, and creative arts. We believe that worship is not just singing songs, but a lifestyle of honoring God with our talents and creating spaces where people can encounter His glory.

Professional worship leading with vocals, instruments, and sound production.

ABOUT US

The Dove Ministries Africa was founded in 2005 by Pastor Daniel and Erica Kaggwa, under the tutelage of Apostle Harry and Deborah Stackhouse. It is located in Nfuufu Zone, Zzana-Bunamwaya in Makindye-Ssabagabo municipality, about 500 metres from the Zzana Roundabout on Entebbe Road.
