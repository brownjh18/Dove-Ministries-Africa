Married Couples

Proverbs 18:22: "He who finds a wife finds what is good and receives favor from the LORD.".

The marriage ministry thrives. Couples gather to strengthen their bond, guided by God's Word and dedicated mentors. Through love, support, and prayer, marriages blossom, and families flourish. The ministry emphasizes communication, forgiveness, and God-centered relationships, enriching the lives of husbands, wives, and their children. In this loving community, marriages are nurtured, celebrating the beauty of God's design for lifelong commitments, as couples walk hand in hand, united in faith and love..

Marriage Enrichment

Dove Church has certified and ordained people to join couples in marriage ceremonies and provide ongoing marriage counseling and support.

ABOUT US

The Dove Ministries Africa was founded in 2005 by Pastor Daniel and Erica Kaggwa, under the tutelage of Apostle Harry and Deborah Stackhouse. It is located in Nfuufu Zone, Zzana-Bunamwaya in Makindye-Ssabagabo municipality, about 500 metres from the Zzana Roundabout on Entebbe Road.
